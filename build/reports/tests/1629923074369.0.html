What is Politrip?

Politrip is an online platform for organizing trips, trainings and webinars about traveling. Politrip also allows travelers to select, view and reserve tours. Politrip is also a platform for organizers of author tours on which they can post their offers and find customers. It is community where organizers and tourists can find each other.
